How do I apply for Academic Consideration?

If you're experiencing illness, injury, or a serious circumstance that affects your ability to meet assessment deadlines or performance, you can apply for Academic Consideration. Follow the steps below to apply:

Step Instruction
Step 1 Visit the UOW Academic Consideration webpage to read the full policy and guidelines.
Step 2 Gather necessary supporting documents, such as medical certificates or other official documentation relevant to your situation.
Step 3 Log in to your SOLS account and navigate to the 'Academic Consideration' section.
Step 4 Fill in the required details, including the subject and the reason for your request.
Step 5 Upload your supporting documents and submit your application for Academic Consideration.
Step 6 Wait for a response. You will be notified via email or your SOLS account about the outcome of your application.

If you need further assistance, feel free to visit the Academic Consideration page or contact student services.
